album.onloop


Content aggregator for Music Albums.

Thanks to Google Search, Allmusic, RateYourMusic, Discogs, iTunes Store, Pitchfork, Sputnikmusic, Rolling Stone, Metacritic, Spotify, Youtube and free information on the Web.

Disclaimer: I DO NOT own the content in the Search results. They belong to, and are fetched from, the aforementioned websites.

In case you missed the Placeholder in the input box, an empty Search returns results on a random album. Also, the more elaborate the Search query is, the more relevant the results. You can also run a search by typing http://album.onloop.net?q=album+name in your address bar. Again, http://album.onloop.net?q=random gives you a random album.

Also, Rateyourmusic seems to block the crawler with a captcha frequently, sorry about that :(
